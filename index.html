About us

  • TruVote Incorporated is a Delaware corporation based in Los Angeles, California. Its purpose is to be an election voting digitized method for any and all political elections done around the globe. The company is comprised of internet, cybersecurity; technical and financial specialist who focuses is the privacy and anonymity of the public and political officials with the strictest of policies and procedures.

  • TruVote is a mobile, web based voting platform which allows precise and secured voting for elections. We provide a secured method with integrity of voting from our proprietary technology. Our technology makes the voting process easier and definite of the selections chosen by registered voters.

  • Our mobile web based voting platform will streamline the method of electing political candidates without the threat of any manipulation from any outside influences once the vote is casted. The vote is secured, permanent and cannot be changed. TruVote will provide ease and accessibility for the disabled, the elderly, first responders, absentee voters and the men and women of the armed forces to cast votes from anywhere in the world. We are providing the political arena with the most secured online voting platform available with emphasis from cybersecurity protocols and TruVote’s proprietary technology for every sector for online voting.

  • Our mobile web based voting platform along with strategic alliances from election divisions throughout multiple countries will further promote our online voting application method. We will have up to the minute election information as elections are being done; in addition to changes in ballot and measure selections days before elections begin. At TruVote we strive to provide the world with an ease of use secure voting method that will have our voting process put democracy back in the hands of the voters. Our voting app will have the potential of creating thought provoking dialogue amongst one another as to where we’re heading as a culture using 21st century technology now and beyond.
